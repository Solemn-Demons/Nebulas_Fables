That really wasn't saying much, he doesn't ever regret anything, but hurting Obi-Wan, that was something to regret. Obi-Wan was gasping for breath, clutching the side that had the light saber wound, and hobbling over towards his light saber at the edge of the platform. "Master don't try it, don't make me have to kill you." Anakin is begging him at this point. He loves Obi-Wan more than anyone else in the galaxy, the universe. Obi-Wan audibly scoffs at him, grabbing his light saber and trying to straighten out as much as possible. "Look what you have done, Anakin! You were supposed to destroy the Sith not join them! Bring balance to the force not leave it in darkness!" This time it's Anakin who scoffs. "Obi-Wan you're mistaken. I haven't joined the Sith, I AM the Sith! I always have been!"
